Goa Pindul menyajikan Cave Tubing yaitu kegiatan menyusuri aliran sungai bawah tanah dengan menggunakan pelampung. Untuk keamanan dan kenyamanan Goa Pindul sudah memenuhi standar operasional dengan fasilitas yang ada Pindul cave is a tourist attraction in the form of a cave with a greenish underground river flow. There are 3 zones that visitors will pass through for 45 minutes. Pindul Cave presents Cave Tubing which is an activity to walk down the underground river using buoys. For the safety and comfort of Goa Pindul has met operational standards with existing facilities
